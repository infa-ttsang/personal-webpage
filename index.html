Current and Recent Projects

University of Pennsylvania Research Intern

I am part of Professor Li Shen's research group in the Department of Biostatistics, Epidemiology, and Informatics. As a lab member, I am constantly applying my computer science skills to questions in healthcare and biotechnology, an area I enjoy exploring. Currently, I am developing a data mining pipeline to identify drug-drug interaction (DDI) patterns from health record databases. I manage these DDI patterns using MongoDB and Node.js and automate the conversion of DDI pattern results to a graphic representation on the web browser using Python.

Informatica Software Engineer Intern

This past summer, I interned at Informatica's Cloud Development group. I automated the end-to-end orchestration of the Informatica Intelligent Cloud Service (IICS) product deployment. My project helped decrease pod deployment time from 3 days to 2 hours, thus saving over $1 million in the pod disaster recovery budget. This experience left me excited and motivated to continue pursuing software opportunities in the future.
